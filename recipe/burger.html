Burger

HOME

Ingredients

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or burger sauce
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix gently â€” do not overwork the meat.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and add butter or oil.
  4. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until browned. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly on the skillet or grill for extra flavor.
  6. Assemble the burger: place the patty on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your preferred sauces.
  7. Cover with the top bun, serve immediately, and enjoy your homemade burger!

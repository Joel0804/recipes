Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce. Perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  3. Cook ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
